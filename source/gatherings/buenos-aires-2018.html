Event Overview


The OpenShift Commons Gathering will be co-located in Austin with CNCF's KubeCon at the Hilton Austin!

The OpenShift Commons Gathering brings together experts from all over the world to discuss container technologies, best practices for cloud native application developers and the open source software projects that underpin the OpenShift ecosystem. The Austin event will gather developers, devops professionals and sysadmins together to explore the next steps in making container technologies successful and secure.
