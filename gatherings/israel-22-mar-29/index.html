OSC TLV 2023 Keynote: Realizing Value from AI/ML, Steven Huels, Director AI/ML Cloud Srvcs, Red Hat

OpenShift Commons Gathering Israel 2023 Language: English Speaker: Steven Huels, Director AI/ML Cloud Services, Red Hat At OpenShift Commons Gathering Israel 2023, Steven Huels discusses the challenges and opportunities of integrating AI/ML into applications. He emphasizes the shift towards open source and the need for repeatable processes, transparency, and governance in AI/ML. The importance of good data, an operational lifecycle, and security in the software supply chain are also discussed. Steven talks about Red Hat OpenShift AI, OpenShift Container Platform and plans to incorporate new capabilities quickly into the platform as a whole. https://commons.openshift.org/join/

OSC TLV 2023: Leading Innovation in Healthcare - Liora Shechter, CIO, Clalit

OpenShift Commons Gathering Israel 2023 Language: English Speaker: Liora Schechter, CIO, Clalit Health Services In this session at OpenShift Commons Gathering Israel 2023, Liora Schechter, CIO of Clalit, discusses leading innovations in healthcare. She highlights the shift from physical encounters to virtual meetings and asynchronous requests, making healthcare more convenient for patients. Liora discusses how Clalit has been working with Red Hat to implement their OpenShift platform and utilize AI tools for personalized and proactive care. She showcases the groundbreaking "home ultrasound" device that allows pregnant women to monitor their baby's health and how it has already saved three lives. With remote monitoring and digital services, Clalit's vision is to develop a holistic and proactive digital healthcare platform. https://commons.openshift.org/join

Event Overview

This OpenShift Commons Gathering will be held on March 29th live in Tel Aviv, Israel

This OpenShift Commons Gathering will be held in-person and all talks will be delivered live. As always, our focus is on creating a welcoming and inclusive space for peer-to-peer interactions online. This Gathering will focus on talks from and by Cloud Native practitioners with production deployments sharing their use cases, insights into their workloads and lessons learned along the way.
